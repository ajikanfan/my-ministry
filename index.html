What is this website for?

For all those things hath mine hand made, and all those things have been, saith the LORD: but to this man will I look, even to him that is poor and of a contrite spirit, and trembleth at my word.Isaiah 66:2 This website is for those who are looking for an answer. Those people who have tried everything, but don't know where else to turn. I want to let you in on a little secret: good poeple don't go to heaven! Believers in the Lord Jesus Christ go to heaven! Jesus isn't interested in your good works, he is only interested in your heart.


When he hung naked on the cross he was thinking of you. It doesn't matter what you've done, we don't care and neither does Jesus. It's important to remember that God is light and in him there is no darkness. So the darker your past or the things you've done, the brighter you'll shine for Jesus. Jesus still heals, he still delivers and he still sets people free. Jesus is the answer! Don't give up on yourself, there is a way and there is help. You can't always control how you start out in life, but you can control the way you finish!

is built upon on sound biblical doctrine. By serving our community with a spirit of excellence and love, we hope to bring the gospel of Jesus Christ to a dying world.
