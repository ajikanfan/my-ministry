A proud subsidiary of Christ Fameland Chapel International

Christ Fameland Chapel International (CFCI) is an international ministry. At you will experience the healing, restoration and breakthrough which comes through applying God’s word to your life.

exists to uplift our community and win souls for Jesus. We are not just a church but a ministry which brings hope to the distressed, joy to the frustrated, healing to the sick and freedom to those in demonic bondage.

is built upon on sound biblical doctrine. By serving our community with a spirit of excellence and love, we hope to bring the gospel of Jesus Christ to a dying world.

Our members come from a rich diversity of racial backgrounds, religious backgrounds, and life experiences. We embrace our differences and utilize them to help each other along our unique paths to becoming loving, joyful people.

We believe in Jesus’ power to transform broken lives into living testimonies through the power of his word and anointing of the Holy Spirit.

Breaking of soul ties

Heavenly Father, in the Name of Jesus I submit my soul, desires and emotions to the Holy Spirit.

I confess to you all of my promiscuous premarital sexual relationships, as well as all sexual relationships outside of marriage as sin. I also confess all ungodly spiritual, soul-ish or fleshly bondage as sin.

I thank you that you have forgiven me, and cleansed me of all unrighteousness through the blood of Jesus. I thank you that you have given me the keys to your kingdom, and spiritual authority. What I bind will be bound, and what I loose will be loosed.

In the Name of Jesus, I ask you to free me from all soul ties with previous sexual partners, and ungodly relationships. Holy Spirit please pull out all these roots, and tentacles of sexual bondage. Cut off the roots of the tree, emotional dependencies, lusts and addictions.

I bind, renounce and rebuke any demons that are strengthening these soul ties, or that I received through past sexual encounters.

I cancel all of your assignments against me in the Name of Jesus and I declare your bondage broken in my life. I will not allow you in my life any further.

I now wield the sword of the Spirit against you and cut off the soul ties between me and the following people...

Father I pray that you will take away all memories that I have of these ungodly unions that I may be free to give my soul fully to you and my partner.

Father I accept your forgiveness for all past sexual sins, and I believe that I have been totally forgiven, and that I am cleansed of all unrighteousness.

I now dedicate myself to you, please keep me holy in soul, spirit and body by your grace. To you belong all the honour and the glory in Jesus name.

In Jesus name, amen!

Prayer to renounce familiar Spirits

Lord Jesus Christ, I belong to you and you have saved me with your precious Blood. I need no other spirits in my life, except the Holy Spirit of God. Please forgive me today for accepting any help, guidance, comfort or information from any other spirits. Lord I also choose now to renounce all spirits that have come down my family line and are still in any way influencing my life. I also lay down and renounce any and all "gifts" or abilities that I have received from these spirits, and I command all such gifting that didn't originate from God to leave me now.

Holy Spirit, You have been sent by God the Father to teach, equip, comfort and guide me in Gods ways. I desperately need you in every area of my life. As Satan's counterfeit gifting releases me, I ask that You would fill me in the areas that they once influenced.

Satan, today I choose to stand firm in the victory that Jesus gained over you on my behalf. I take authority over every spirit that came down my bloodline from my ancestors. In the mighty name of Jesus Christ my Lord and Saviour I now command those spirits to leave me. Leave now!

In Jesus name, amen!

Prayer for release from Jezebel

ather, I come to you in the name of Jesus Christ the Shepard of my soul. I confess and renounce the sin in my life through which I manipulated, dominated and controlled other people.

I hate, renounce and reject the defeated Jezebel spirit and claim my victory against it in the name of Jesus (Ps. 139).

Because Jesus died for me on the cross, and became a curse on my behalf I now declare that every curse of Jezebel is broken up to seven generations before me on both sides of my family.

Father I also ask you to break any ties that I might still have with those that practice witchcraft against me.

Above all Lord please help me to fully submit my life to you.

Jesus said, "They that believe in me will cast out devils".

I believe in Jesus therefore I can take authority over every demon associated with Jezebel and command them to go from me in Jesus name!

In Jesus name, amen!

Prayer to renounce the occult

Thank you Lord Jesus that you have died for my sins.

Thank you Jesus for your glorious resurrection, and that you have made me a new creature through my faith in the cleansing power of your precious blood.

Lord Jesus, I confess the following before you today:

  • I sought supernatural experiences that were outside of your will, and I was disobedient to your Word.
  • I ask you Lord to help me to renounce these things, and to heal me in body, soul and spirit in Jesus mighty name.
  • I renounce all witchcraft and sorcery, both black and white.
  • I renounce the ouija board, playing with "glassy", book and key, dungeons and dragons and all other occultic games.
  • I renounce all spiritualistic sessions, clairvoyance, and all spiritualistic mediums.
  • I renounce ESP, second sight and all mind reading.
  • I renounce all forms of fortune telling, palm reading, crystal balls, card reading etc.
  • I renounce all interest and contact with astrology and horoscopes.
  • I renounce reincarnation, and all contact with divination healing groups.
  • I renounce hypnotism under any circumstances.
  • I renounce all interest in the past, or the future that is outside of your will.
  • I renounce dowsing, devil worship and automatic writing.
  • I also renounce all literature that I have read in the above mentioned areas.
  • I now break in the name of Jesus all hereditary demonic influence over my family line due to my ancestors involvement in any of the above mentioned areas.
  • I renounce every cult that renounces the Blood of Jesus.
  • I renounce every philosophy that questions the divinity of Christ, or that renounces the Holy Spirit.

I now call on the name of Jesus to set me free!

Lord I have another confession to make: I have harboured bitterness and resentment towards others.

Lord, please help me now to forgive them: I now choose to forgive the following people... & I also forgive myself!

I now renounce all evil spirits that are tormenting me, and I command you to leave me now in the name of Jesus!

Salvation prayer

Lord Jesus Christ, I believe that you are the Son of God, and the only way to God. I also believe that you died on the cross and laid down your life for all sinners. Lord, today I confess to You that I am a sinner, and that I have sinned against you. Lord please have mercy on me and forgive me all of my sins. Today I accept your selfless sacrifice on the cross on my behalf, and I accept You as my Lord and Saviour. Lord I invite You today to come and live in my heart, and I give You my life. I thank You now for Your forgiveness Lord, and from this day I choose to live for you.

In Jesus name, amen!

This is the first, and most important step for any person that needs healing or deliverance from Satan's bondage in their lives. Welcome! Welcome to the most powerful, exciting phenomenal Kingdom in all the universe, God will bless you!

Prayer to renounce witchcraft or Satanism

I renounce all vows or oaths that I made or were made on my behalf at the altar of Satan whether known or unknown to me in the name of Jesus Christ. Lord Jesus, I ask you today to please destroy every contract or oath or vow or ritual that I partook of whether through Satanism, witchcraft, the occult or spiritism through your precious blood. I believe in your death and resurrection on the cross, and I pray that your victory over Satan on the cross would become my victory over Satan to defeat all his power in my life.

I renounce all inherited curses of spirits affecting me through my ancestor's involvement in witchcraft and pray that the Lord Jesus would cleanse my bloodline through his precious blood. I nail all these things to the cross.

Because of Jesus' blood Satan has no more power over me or my family. I renounce him and all his demons and declare them my enemies. In Jesus name I take up my Godly authority and expel all evil spirits thereby destroying completely all their power in my life.

In the name of Jesus I remove all Satan's right to influence me or my kids from this moment on. In Jesus authority I bind all demons in my life and command them to go to where the Holy Spirit sends them!

Father in the name of Jesus I pray that you would close every doorway that I ever opened to Satan through sin in my life. Please shut them through the blood of Jesus. I thank you for this in Jesus name.

I place my past behind me, and choose God's future for my life from this day forward.

In Jesus name, amen!
